Services

Supporting people with mental health issues to live fulfilling lives in a homely environment.

24/7 Support

Seabank House operates two fully staffed care homes (Seabank House and Aberlea House) in Aberdeen, providing round-the-clock support for vulnerable adults with mental health issues.

Mental Health Support

Tailored support for individuals experiencing mental health conditions, helping them manage symptoms, engage in therapeutic and wellness activities, and work toward recovery.

Skills Assistance

Helping residents with daily tasks such as preparing for and attending appointments, doing domestic chores, organising personal finances & benefits, medication management

Community Integration

Opportunities for social engagement and therapy: social therapy, activities to build confidence, social inclusion, connecting with external services, outings

Safe Environment

Provision of safe accommodation, communal living, meals, communal areas, meals, rooms, etc. An environment where people can focus on recovery with support from staff

Personalised Care

Every resident has an individual plan that reflects their needs, preferences, goals; staff work with them to review and adapt as needed.
