Supported Living for Mental Health in Aberdeen

Seabank House provides 24/7 residential support and assistance for adults living with mental health issues.

Across two locations in the heart of Aberdeen, we offer a calm, homely environment where individuals are supported to live with dignity, build confidence and move toward greater independence at their own pace.

A registered charity that has been supporting the local community for 100+ years, Seabank House stands for inclusion, encouragement and supporting fulfilling lives.

Rooter in Community

Seabank House has been embedded in its community for 100+ years, offering a sense of belonging and continuity for those it supports and works with.

Its small scale, independence and long-standing presence make it a trusted part of residents’ lives, and this deep-rooted trust is integral to its brand identity.
