Supported Living for Mental Health in Aberdeen

Seabank House provides 24/7 residential support and assistance for adults living with mental health issues.

A registered charity that has been supporting the local community for 100+ years, Seabank House stands for inclusion, encouragement and supporting fulfilling lives.

Rooted in Community

Across two locations in the heart of Aberdeen, we offer a calm, homely environment where individuals are supported to live with dignity, build confidence and move toward greater independence at their own pace.

Seabank House has been embedded in its community for 100+ years, offering a sense of belonging and continuity for those it supports and works with.

Its small scale, independence and long-standing presence make it a trusted part of residents’ lives, and this deep-rooted trust is integral to its brand identity.

How You Can Make A Difference

Your support helps Seabank House provide vulnerable adults in Aberdeen with the safe spaces, guidance, and opportunities they need to build confidence, develop life skills, and move toward greater independence. Here's what you can do.

Volunteer

Give your time and make a meaningful impact at Seabank House Aberdeen. Whether it’s offering companionship or lending a helping hand, your presence can brighten lives and strengthen our community.

Donate

Your generosity helps us continue providing care and support to those who need it most. Every contribution, big or small, makes a lasting difference—help us keep our doors open and our services strong.

Work With Us

Join a dedicated team committed to making a positive difference every day. At Seabank House Aberdeen, your skills and compassion will help create a supportive and caring environment for everyone we serve.
