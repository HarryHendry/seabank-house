About Us

Mission

At Seabank House, our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and homely environment where people living with mental health challenges can build independence, develop life skills, and live fulfilling lives.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every individual is valued, respected, and given the opportunity to grow in confidence and wellbeing, with the right support at every step.

Our Values

  • Compassion - treating every person with kindness, dignity, and understanding.
  • Independence - empowering individuals to make choices and shape their own lives.
  • Community - fostering connections, belonging, and mutual support.
  • Respect - recognising the unique strengths and potential in everyone.
